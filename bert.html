Bert is Ernie's best friend and roommate on Sesame Street. The pair share the basement apartment at 123 Sesame Street. In contrast to the practical-joking, extroverted Ernie, Bert is serious, studious, and tries to make sense of his friend's actions.
His own passions include reading Boring Stories, collecting paper clips and bottle caps (especially the rare Figgy Fizz), consuming oatmeal, and studying pigeons.

Interesting facts: Unfortunately, Bert's nose has fallen off several times during the course of the series. He is left-handed.
Bert and Ernie were the first muppets to appear in the pilot episode of Sesame Street. He made one appearance on The Muppet Show: in episode #102 (where he sang Some Enchanted Evening) and in episode #518.
Bert was involved as a director in Elmopalooza.
